Reading the heatmap

Retention is consistently strong through month 2 (onboarding rarely loses sellers immediately), then bends downward from month 4–6 as low-fit sellers churn out — the window the health model below is built to catch early.

FMCG cohorts retain best at the 3-month checkpoint, consistent with lower return rates and more repeatable, low-consideration purchases — a useful data point for prioritizing FMCG brand incubation.
